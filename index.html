Hi, I'm Rounak

Senior Software Engineer @ Urban Company • Chevening Scholar • King's College London

I am a senior software engineer with Urban Company (formerly known as Urban Clap). At UC, I work with the financial tech team ensuring seamless payments and payouts for our customers and providers. I take an active lead on the partner payout ecosystem (reconciliations, bank transfers, advances, incentives) ensuring high levels of satisfaction for our providers. I actively contribute to infrastructure projects such as access control, audit systems to name a few.

Prior to my current role, I was a product engineer at Gram Vaani working actively to intelligently digitize immunization data across India using computer vision techniques. Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation along with BIRAC supported this project.

I graduated from King's College London as a Chevening Scholar under Dr. Nishanth Sastry. I have previously led two organization in Kolkata - Digital Bongs and E Info Solutions.

I am interested in exploring finance flows at scale. I enjoy transforming super complex real world problems into simple digital solutions.
